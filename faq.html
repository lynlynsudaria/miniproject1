How much is the minimum purchase of cosmetics products to avail the free delivery?

We offer a free delivery service with a minimum purchase of Php 500, for areas within Northern Mindanao, and Php 1000, for areas outside Northern Mindanao, worth of cosmetic products such as whitening soap, gluta lotion, facial mask, foot mask, whitening capsule, white serum, white day cream, lip tint, body scrub, balm, collagen cream, acne solution, intimate cleansing, facial cleanser, peeling gel and other cosmetics product.

How much is your delivery fee inside Northern Mindanao?

We have free delivery within Northern Mindanao? if your purchase is above 500 pesos, if its below 500 pesos then your delivery fee is 100 pesos.

How much is your delivery fee outside Northern Mindanao?

We have free delivery ouside Northern Mindanao if your purchase exceeds 1,000 pesos if not then your delivery fee is 190 pesos.
